<h4><strong>Application to Transfer Out from St. Margaret’s School (Secondary)&nbsp;</strong></h4>
<p>Secondary 1 students who have appealed successfully for school transfer,
will need to submit the ”Acceptance Form for School Transfer” used by the <strong>receiving school</strong> to
the General Office at St. Margaret’s School (Secondary). Please contact
Ms Doreen Owyong at 64664525 to make an appointment to come to the school.
